Privacy Policy for CFC CRM

Last updated: July 2024

Overview

CFC CRM is a Chrome extension that helps you sync your LinkedIn connections to your application.

Information We Collect

  • LinkedIn Connection Data: Names, companies, positions, and profile URLs from your LinkedIn connections
  • Authentication Tokens: JWT tokens for secure API communication
  • User Profile Information: Basic profile data from your authentication system

How We Use Information

  • Sync Connections: Transfer your LinkedIn connections to your backend application
  • Authentication: Verify your identity using JWT tokens
  • Improve Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Authentication tokens stored locally in your browser
  • Your Backend: All connection data is sent to your own backend server
  • No External Servers: We do not store your data on our servers

Data Security

  • Encrypted Communication: All API calls use HTTPS
  • Local Processing: Connection extraction happens locally in your browser
  • Secure Tokens: JWT tokens are stored securely in browser storage

Third-Party Services

  • LinkedIn: We access your LinkedIn connections page
  • Your Backend: Data is sent to your specified backend server
  • No Other Services: We do not share data with third parties

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all data processed by the extension
  • Delete: Remove the extension to stop all data processing
  • Control: You control which connections to sync

Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: your-email@example.com

Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Users will be notified of significant changes.

Note: This extension requires you to run your own backend server. All data processing and storage is under your control.
